Q: What is StayNest?
A: StayNest is a homestay rental platform where hosts can rent rooms to guests.

Q: How can I list my property?
A: Sign up as a host, complete KYC, and add your property details.

Q: Does StayNest support cancellation?
A: Yes, you can cancel bookings through your dashboard.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: You can reach our 24/7 support through the Help section or via email.
